Core Features

Everything a Medical
Student Needs

From AI-powered parsing to task tracking — MedHub handles the chaos so you can focus on medicine.

🤖
AI Announcement Parser
Paste any complex announcement text. Groq AI (llama-3.3-70b) instantly extracts structured tasks with deadlines and priorities.
✅
Smart Task Management
Organize, track, and complete tasks with priority levels and deadlines. Never miss an important submission again.
👤
Personalized Experience
Your name and preferences are saved locally. MedHub greets you personally every time you open the app.
📱
14 Beautiful Screens
A complete mobile experience with a consistent teal color scheme, smooth navigation, and clean UI design.
⚡
Live Backend
Powered by a Node.js backend deployed on Render, ensuring real-time AI responses and reliable performance.
🔒
Secure by Design
API keys stored securely on the server. All communications encrypted via HTTPS. Your data stays safe.
